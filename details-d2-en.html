Birthday Adventure

Take them to the Postojna Adventure Park, which is perfect for this occasion. Children will turn the forest setting into a playground full of adventures and new discoveries, which they'll encounter through over 90 different fun obstacles. When they start to get a little tired, they’ll recharge with snacks and refresh themselves with iced tea, so they’ll be ready for new challenges again.

The children will definitely remember this birthday for a long time.



The Birthday Adventure includes:

  • an adventure in the Postojna Adventure Park (2 and a half hours)

    snacks

    ice tea

    animation and supervised guidance in the park (parents can relax)


Parental presence during the celebration is not required, as our awesome animators ensure the safety of all participating children. Of course, you can invite other parents too and try the adventures yourselves.
