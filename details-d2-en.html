Birthday Adventure

Take them to Adventure Park Postojna — it's the perfect place! The kids will turn the forest setting into a playground full of adventures and discoveries as they tackle over 78 different fun obstacles. When they start to run out of energy, they’ll be treated to some delicious pizza and recharge with a burst of new energy. Yum!

It’ll be a birthday they’ll remember for a long time.



The Birthday Adventure includes:

  • Adventure in Adventure Park Postojna / 2.5 hours

    Birthday pizzas

    Iced tea

    Animation and supervised guidance in the park (parents can relax)


Parental presence during the party is not required, as our awesome staff will take care of the safety of all participating children.

Of course, you’re welcome to invite other parents and join in on the adventure yourself!

Price List

  • Single Entry

    Each additional child: €19

Payment is accepted in cash only.

The entry ticket is not time-limited. In case of a large number of visitors, we may kindly ask you to finish your adventure after 3 hours.
Companions, walkers, curious visitors, and dogs do not need a ticket.

Minimum child height with arms extended above the head: 140 cm.
Children under 16 years may enter the adventure park only when accompanied by an adult!
1 adult can supervise a maximum of 3 children.
We recommend wearing sports shoes and clothing.

Prices are subject to change without prior notice.
