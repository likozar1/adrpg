About the Park

A unique, primal, and thrilling walk among the forest treetops, where you’ll test your resourcefulness and adventurous spirit and experience a truly special day. All young people and the young at heart can enjoy a forest adventure made up of ziplines, bridges, ladders, nets, ropes, and wires — climbing or gliding from one adventure to another. With a bit of encouragement, everyone — regardless of age or physical condition — can overcome 90 fun obstacles spread across a large area dedicated to active outdoor fun, socializing, and play, located right next to the Postojna Cave.

Why Spend a Day at the Adventure Park?

Postojna Adventure Park is more than just fun — it’s a chance to connect with nature, meet new friends, and enjoy a safe and well-organized playground where friendly instructors and animators are always ready to help.

Safety

The entire Adventure Park is built in accordance with EU standard EN 15567-1, ensuring 100% safety for all visitors. Your safety is guaranteed by our specially trained instructors, supported by top-quality equipment from Black Diamond, Kong, and Petzl.

We use the CouDuo Pro safety system by Kong, which functions without the need to unclip, eliminating user error or negligence.
