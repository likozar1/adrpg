Adventure Park

Do you want an unforgettable experience surrounded by nature? A day full of adventures, laughter, and fun with friends, family, or colleagues?

About the Park

A unique, primal, and exciting walk among the treetops where you'll test your resourcefulness, adventurous spirit, and experience a truly special day. All the young and young at heart can enjoy a forest adventure made up of bridges, ladders, nets, ropes, wires... which you’ll climb or glide across from one adventure to the next. With a bit of encouragement, everyone – regardless of age or physical fitness – can overcome 78 different fun obstacles located in a large area dedicated to active leisure, socializing, playing, and connecting, right next to the Postojna Cave.

What is an Adventure Park?

The Postojna Adventure Park is designed for fun for the whole family. In the park, you can rise from the ground into the treetops and embark on a true journey among the trees. You'll see and experience the world from a whole new perspective.

The concept of the adventure park is based on an experiential journey across a specially designed climbing course built among the tree canopies, where visitors can choose a difficulty level that suits them. The Postojna Adventure Park consists of 5 different adventurous routes with 78 different climbing elements that offer an exciting and interesting experience. The climbing park provides 2–3 hours of unique fun. Everyone taller than 120 cm or at least 4 years old is welcome.

Why spend a day at the Adventure Park?

The Postojna Adventure Park is more than just fun – it’s about socializing in nature and meeting new friends. It's a safe and well-maintained playground, where friendly instructors and entertainers await you.

Safety

The entire Adventure Park is built in compliance with EU standard EN 15567-1, ensuring 100% safety for all visitors. Your safety is overseen by our specially trained instructors, and you'll be equipped with top-quality gear from brands like Black Diamond, Salewa, and Petzl.
