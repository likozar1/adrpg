TERMS OF USE

Before using the Postojna Adventure Park, every participant must read these rules. By signing, the participant confirms that they are familiar with and agree to the rules. Participants under 15 years of age must be informed of the rules by an adult, who must confirm this with their signature (the name and surname of the responsible adult and participants must be stated, along with contact information). By signing, the participant acknowledges participation in the Adventure Park activities at their own risk.

Use of the Postojna Adventure Park carries certain risks and is undertaken at the participant’s own responsibility. For the liability of Koren Sports d.o.o. and the operator, refer to point 7.

The Adventure Park Postojna is suitable for participants older than 4 years who do not have physical or mental issues related to height that could pose a health or other risk to themselves or others. Children under the age of 15 may use the adventure park only under the supervision of a responsible adult. For the use of individual courses/trails of various difficulty levels, additional requirements apply: **ALL HEIGHTS ARE MEASURED WITH ARMS FULLY EXTENDED ABOVE THE HEAD!**

  • YELLOW

    All participants from the age of 4

  • ORANGE

    All from the age of 6 or 140 cm (with arms fully extended above the head)

  • GREEN

    All from the age of 8 or 160 cm (with arms fully extended above the head)

  • BLUE

    All from the age of 8 or 160 cm (with arms fully extended above the head)

  • RED

    All from the age of 8 or 160 cm (with arms fully extended above the head)

  • BLACK

    All from the age of 10 or 170 cm (with arms fully extended above the head)

Small items such as jewelry, mobile phones, cameras, etc., are not allowed during use of the Adventure Park, as they may pose a danger to the participant or others.

Every participant must attend the safety instruction session, which includes both theoretical and practical parts. All instructions from the trainer/instructor or organizer are mandatory and must be followed. In case of violation, the participant may be required to leave the Adventure Park. For any breaches of safety instructions, Koren Sports d.o.o. and the operator assume no liability for resulting claims.

The safety equipment provided to participants—consisting of a harness and carabiners—must not be removed or transferred to others during use of the park. Equipment must be used as per the instructor’s or organizer’s guidelines. All equipment must be returned after use. Carabiners must always be attached to the safety loop on the harness. At least one carabiner must always be connected to the safety cable. Both carabiners must never be detached from the safety line at the same time. If in doubt, visitors must immediately contact an instructor for clarification.

Koren Sports d.o.o. and the Adventure Park Postojna operator are liable for personal injury within legal regulations. They are liable for material or property damage only in cases of gross negligence by the organizer or authorized persons.

Only one person may perform an activity at a time. A maximum of two people are allowed on a platform simultaneously. Courses may only be used in accordance with the rules outlined in point 3.

Management reserves the right to ask anyone not following the rules to leave the Adventure Park immediately. Management also reserves the right to suspend activities due to safety or technical reasons (e.g., fire, storm, lightning, etc.). In such cases, admission fees are non-refundable. Refunds are also not given if a participant leaves the park voluntarily.

Use of the park under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or other substances that impair physical or mental ability is strictly prohibited. Smoking is not allowed while using the park or wearing safety equipment.

Use of the Adventure Park Postojna outside of opening hours is strictly prohibited!

On the GREEN and BLUE courses, children under 12 years may climb only with adult supervision! On the RED and BLACK courses, children under 15 years may climb only with adult supervision!
