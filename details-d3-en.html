Super Adventure Day

Organize a super adventure day at the Postojna Adventure Park!

Delight schoolchildren with an educational and safe adventure, and awaken their love for nature. Bring them to the Postojna Adventure Park, where we will organize a sports day or end-of-year trip for you. We will safely guide your students among the treetops, where they will discover—through various exciting obstacles—that learning educational content and developing motor skills can also be a lot of FUN.



We offer programs for

  • Preschool children

    Children in the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd triads of primary school

    High school groups


In our park, experienced instructors will take care of your students, ensuring that all activities are completely safe. This way, teachers and parents can be entirely worry-free about the children's safety!

We have over 15 years of experience in organizing and implementing sports and nature-based programs. As the first in Slovenia, we have incorporated educational content into such programs, offering children learning through play.

We organize guided programs for organized school groups. We provide professional instructor-guides who supervise activities in the park, so it is not necessary to provide adult chaperones.

Price List

  • Preschool children and 1st–3rd grade primary school

    Price per child: €14

    Postojna Adventure Park – The program lasts 2 hours.
    Maximum number of children: 25 for preschoolers and 80 for primary school.

  • 4th–9th grade primary school and high school students

    Price per child: €18

    Postojna Adventure Park – The program lasts 2 hours and 30 minutes.
    Maximum number of children: 120


All programs for organized school groups are guided! We provide professional instructor-guides who supervise all activities in the park, so adult chaperones are not required.
As organizers, we reserve the right to terminate the program in the event of serious violations of safety rules or behavior guidelines in the Adventure Park, to ensure the safety of all participants.
