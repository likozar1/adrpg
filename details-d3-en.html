Super Adventure Day

Organize an unforgettable adventure day at Adventure Park Postojna!

Treat schoolchildren to an educational and safe adventure and spark a love for nature. Bring them to Adventure Park Postojna, where we’ll organize a sports or end-of-year excursion for you. We will safely guide your students through the treetops, where they’ll encounter fun and exciting obstacles while discovering that learning educational content and developing physical skills can be FUN.



Our programs are available for:

  • Preschool children

    Elementary school children (1st, 2nd, and 3rd triad)

    High school groups


In our park, your students will be cared for by experienced instructors to ensure that all activities are completely safe — so teachers and parents can be COMPLETELY WORRY-FREE!

We have over 15 years of experience in organizing and running sports and nature-based programs. We were the first in Slovenia to include pedagogical content in our activities and offer kids a chance to learn through play.

We offer guided programs for organized school groups. All activities are supervised by professional instructors, so additional adult supervision is not required.

Price List

  • Preschool & Grades 1–3

    Price per child: €14

    Adventure Park Postojna program duration: 2 hours.
    Max group size: 25 preschoolers / 50 elementary school children

  • Grades 4–9 & High School Students

    Price per child: €18

    Adventure Park Postojna program duration: 2.5 hours.
    Max group size: 120 children


All programs for organized school groups are guided!
We provide professional instructors who supervise all park activities — additional adult supervision is not required.
As the organizer, we reserve the right to cancel the program in cases of gross violations of safety rules or inappropriate behavior, in order to ensure the safety of all children.


For more information, call us at: 040 567 046
or email us at: info@pustolovski-park-postojna.si

Payment is accepted in cash only.

The entrance fee is not time-limited. In case of a high number of visitors, we may kindly ask you to conclude your adventure after 3 hours.
Companions, walkers, curious onlookers, and dogs do not need to pay for entry.

Minimum child height with arms extended above the head: 140 cm.
Children under 16 years of age may enter the Adventure Park only when accompanied by an adult!
One adult may supervise up to 3 children.
We recommend wearing sports shoes and clothing.

Prices are subject to change without prior notice.
