Family Adventure

Just as elders in the animal kingdom teach the younger ones how to fight and survive, it's a good idea to do the same with your own young family members in modern times—especially before they move out and get jobs. The Adventure Park Postojna offers a great location to test your children's skills and resourcefulness. Swing like a monkey on a rope, climb logs like a bear, and roar like a lion! This will be the ultimate family outing.

Adventure Park Postojna invites you to a family fun day!

The Adventure Park is a large area dedicated to active leisure time, play, and bonding with your children—and others. Being in nature enhances the survival instinct and has a positive effect on the body. The park is especially designed for family visits, with equipment and fun obstacles that offer entertainment for the whole family.

Children from the age of 4 can move independently through the obstacle course with the help of experienced instructors. They will try ziplining, sliding, climbing, walking, jumping, meet peers, and experience the forest and nature in a whole new way. The skills you gain along the way will also help you better handle your daily parenting challenges—or you can simply observe and cheer them on in their adventure.
