Climbing Routes

The Adventure Park Postojna will surprise you with 6 climbing routes and as many as 70 climbing elements!

The Adventure Park Postojna consists of 6 different adventure paths with 70 various climbing elements, offering an interesting and exciting adventure. The climbing park provides a unique 2-3 hour entertainment experience.

  • YELLOW

    All participants from 4 years old and up

  • ORANGE

    All from 6 years old or over 140 cm (with arms extended above the head)

  • GREEN

    All from 8 years old or 160 cm (with arms extended above the head)

  • BLUE

    All from 8 years old or 160 cm (with arms extended above the head)

  • RED

    All from 8 years old or 160 cm (with arms extended above the head)

  • BLACK

    All from 10 years old or 170 cm (with arms extended above the head)

On the GREEN and BLUE courses, children under 12 years old can climb only when accompanied by an adult! On the RED and BLACK courses, children under 15 years old can climb only when accompanied by an adult!
