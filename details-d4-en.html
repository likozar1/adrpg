Well-Tuned Team of Adventurers

Want to strengthen team spirit, collaboration skills, and group synergy in a cheerful and relaxed natural setting?

Our teambuilding program is designed for business teams looking to develop creativity, strengthen unity, and highly motivate participants through a professionally guided experience. Participants will overcome various obstacles and team challenges, pushing their limits and ultimately becoming a well-coordinated team.

The program includes cooperative games and activities in the adventure park.

  • The entire duration of the event includes access to a group seating area with tables and benches (capacity: 60 people).

    We can also arrange food and drinks for your group event upon request.


Programs are carried out in collaboration with Koren Sports.
