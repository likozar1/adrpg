Independent Entry

POSTOJNA ADVENTURE PARK offers you a unique, primal, and thrilling walk among the forest treetops where you will test your resourcefulness, adventurous spirit, and experience a truly special day.

All young people and the young at heart are invited to a forest adventure made up of bridges, ladders, nets, ropes, wires... through which you'll climb or glide from one adventure to the next.

With a bit of encouragement, everyone—regardless of age or physical fitness—can overcome 90 different fun obstacles located in a large area dedicated to active leisure, socializing, playing, and spending time together, just a stone’s throw from the Postojna Cave.

Children under the age of 16 may enter the adventure park only when accompanied by an adult!
1 adult may accompany a maximum of 3 children!
